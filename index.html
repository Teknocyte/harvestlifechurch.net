LEADERSHIP

Lee and Janet Frost
Pastor

Pastor Lee and Janet Frost have led Harvest Life as pastors for 18 years. Pastor Lee's strong belief lies in God's capacity for breakthroughs, restoration, and healing in our lives. He emphasizes achieving this through studying God's Word, prayer, and applying His truths and promises, as outlined in John 10:10, for an abundant life. Pastor Lee is convinced that with faith, individuals can overcome any challenge the enemy presents, leading to victorious and prosperous lives. Having experienced the transformative power of God in his own life, Pastor Lee is committed to helping others witness Christ's miraculous influence in their daily experiences, allowing them to embrace lives of freedom, victory, and prosperity, as assured in God's Word.

Wade and Kim Brasher
Associate Pastor

Wade Brasher, associate pastor at Harvest Life, and his wife Kim are dedicated servants in various ministry roles. Wade's spiritual journey began at age 13 when he embraced the gospel, and at 16, he experienced the Baptism of the Holy Spirit, sensing a divine calling. He initially led youth choirs and later became a worship leader in multiple Alabama churches, eventually transitioning to a teaching role with a prophetic mantle. Kim, a registered nurse at Grandview, has a lifelong ministry history, including membership in the nationally touring singing group, The Maharrey Family. Since their marriage, they've been actively involved in praise and worship ministry, with Kim also overseeing the creative vision and production of annual Christmas events. Their children, Jordyn and Joel, play essential roles in various ministries, including nursery service, dance team participation, and Joel's responsibilities as a drummer and youth teacher at Faith School.
